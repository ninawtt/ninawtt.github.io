We've been friends for

A story of two girls - Christy and Nina.

We met in high school. Since then, we've been good friends.
We share everything in our lives, no matter what happened - happy or sorrow.
We like the same singers. We have the same values of money, life and relationship.
We have the same last name. We even list each other as one of our relatives on Facebook.
We call each other "Honey" even I'm already married.
We both know that we always are supported by each other.
In particular, Christy went to Australia in 2013 and I came to Canada since 2018.
We used to hang out frequently when we were both in Taiwan.
We never forget each other's birthday.
As I'm currently learning Computer Science and being 12,000 km away from Christy,
I design this website as a gift for her and wish her all the best.
