About Brian

I am an experienced real estate agent who got into the business after purchasing and selling a couple of houses because I realized that clients deserved a better experience than what most realtors were offering. For the amount of commissions collected today in real estate, you should expect exceptional service in return. I include a comprehensive marketing package to help you sell your property for the highest amount possible and give honest advice when purchasing a home so that we might find the perfect home for you﻿. I am a hard-working and honest person who tries to give back to my community, as well.

Top 3 reasons to work with me

  • I provide a full suite of marketing services including professional photography, video, staging advice, and professional feature sheets.
  • I provide honest advice and will not pressure you to purchase or sell a property unless the terms are right for you.
  • I provide a wealth of comprehensive data to help you determine the market value for the property you are buying or selling.
